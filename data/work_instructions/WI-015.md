# WI-015: Gauge R&R Study Execution

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the steps necessary for conducting a Gauge Repeatability and Reproducibility (R&R) study. This study is essential to ensure measurement systems meet the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) requirements, specifically per AIQS 3.5.4, and to enhance product quality.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the measurement system analysis within the manufacturing plant. It encompasses the use of equipment asset IDs 2847.310.0061 (Vertical Turning Lathe) and 2847.310.0042 (Horizontal CNC Machining Center) in the execution of Gauge R&R studies as part of the quality assurance process.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel shall complete an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) prior to executing the Gauge R&R study. Ensure that all measuring instruments are calibrated and in compliance with AIQS 4.4.1. Review previous Nonconformance Reports (NCRs) related to measurement systems to identify potential issues. Personnel must also be trained in measurement techniques and familiar with the First Article Inspection (FAI) process. Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures must be followed to ensure safety during equipment operation.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Gather all necessary tools and measuring devices. Verify that the Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061) and Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) are operational and have passed the ORR.
   
2. **Select Parts**: Choose a representative sample of parts for the study. Ensure that the selected parts reflect the range of variability expected in production.

3. **Conduct Measurements**: Assign multiple operators to measure the same parts using the selected gauge. Each operator shall perform measurements independently to assess both repeatability and reproducibility.

4. **Data Collection**: Record all measurement data systematically. Ensure that data is collected in accordance with AIQS 3.1.4, which mandates proper documentation practices.

5. **Analysis**: Analyze the collected data to calculate the Gauge R&R percentage. Compare results against the acceptable limits defined in AIQS 4.4.2. If the results indicate unacceptable variation, initiate corrective actions and document findings.

6. **Reporting**: Compile a report summarizing the Gauge R&R study findings. Include recommendations for improvement if necessary, and submit the report to the quality assurance team for review.

## 5. Verification
Post-execution, the quality assurance team shall verify the Gauge R&R study results against the AIQS standards. Ensure that all findings are documented and any NCRs are addressed promptly. Follow up on corrective actions to confirm that measurement systems are functioning within acceptable limits as per AIQS 4.4.1.
