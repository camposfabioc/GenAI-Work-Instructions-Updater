# WI-010: Process Parameter Adjustment Procedure

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the procedure for adjusting process parameters on equipment to ensure optimal performance and compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This procedure aims to minimize variations and maintain product quality, thereby reducing the likelihood of Nonconformance Reports (NCRs) per AIQS 3.2.1.

## 2. Scope
This procedure applies to all operators and technicians involved in the operation of the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061) and the Injection Molding Unit (Asset ID: 2847.420.0023). It encompasses the adjustment of process parameters necessary for achieving desired product specifications as defined in the AIQS.

## 3. Prerequisites
Before commencing this procedure, personnel shall complete the Operational Readiness Review (ORR) for the respective equipment. Personnel must also be trained in Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures to ensure safety during adjustments. Review the current process parameters and ensure that all necessary tools and equipment are available. Additionally, verify that the equipment has passed the First Article Inspection (FAI) as per AIQS 2.1.2. Confirm that no NCRs are outstanding for the equipment being adjusted per AIQS 3.2.2.

## 4. Procedure Step 1
Begin by reviewing the current process parameters for the Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061). Access the equipment's control panel and navigate to the parameter settings. Document the existing parameters, including spindle speed, feed rate, and coolant flow rate. Ensure that all readings are within the acceptable range as defined in the equipment's operational manual. If any parameters are outside the specified limits, prepare to adjust them in accordance with AIQS 3.2.4. Notify the supervisor of any discrepancies before proceeding.

## 5. Procedure Step 2
After documenting the current parameters, initiate the Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure to ensure the equipment is safely shut down. Confirm that all energy sources are isolated and locked out. Once LOTO is complete, proceed to adjust the necessary parameters on the Vertical Turning Lathe. Use the control panel to modify the spindle speed and feed rate as required. Ensure that adjustments are made gradually, monitoring the effects on the equipment's operation. Follow the guidelines set forth in AIQS 2.1.4 to maintain compliance.

## 6. Procedure Step 3
Next, conduct a test run of the Vertical Turning Lathe with the adjusted parameters. Observe the machine's performance closely, checking for any unusual sounds or vibrations. Record the results of the test run, including any deviations from expected performance metrics. If the machine operates within acceptable limits, proceed to the next step. If not, revert to the original settings and reassess the adjustments made. Document any issues encountered during the test run as per AIQS 2.2.1.

## 7. Procedure Step 4
Once the test run is successful, repeat the process for the Injection Molding Unit (2847.420.0023). Access the control panel and review the current process parameters. Document the existing settings, including injection speed, temperature, and cycle time. Adjust the parameters as necessary, ensuring compliance with the specifications outlined in AIQS 3.3.3. After adjustments, implement the LOTO procedure to safely shut down the equipment before making any changes.

## 8. Procedure Step 5
Conduct a test run of the Injection Molding Unit following the parameter adjustments. Monitor the machine's performance closely, checking for consistency and quality of the molded parts. Ensure that the output meets the specifications defined in the quality standards. Document the results of the test run and any observations made during the process. If the results are satisfactory, finalize the adjustments. If issues arise, revert to the previous settings and investigate potential causes per AIQS 2.3.1.

## 9. Verification
After completing the adjustments and test runs for both pieces of equipment, verify that all documented parameters align with the required specifications. Conduct a final review of the adjustments with a supervisor to ensure compliance with AIQS standards. If any discrepancies are found, initiate corrective actions as necessary. Ensure that all adjustments are logged in the equipment maintenance records for future reference.

## 10. Records
Maintain records of all parameter adjustments, test run results, and any issues encountered during the process. These records shall be stored in the equipment maintenance log and made available for review during audits. Ensure that all documentation is completed in accordance with AIQS 3.2.1 and is accessible for future reference.
