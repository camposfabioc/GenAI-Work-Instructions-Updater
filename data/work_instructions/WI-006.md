# WI-006: Hydraulic Press Die Changeover

## 1. Purpose  
This Work Instruction (WI) outlines the procedure for changing dies on the hydraulic press to ensure compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) and maintain operational efficiency. Proper die changeover is critical to prevent defects and ensure product quality as per AIQS 3.3.1.

## 2. Scope  
This procedure applies to all operators and maintenance personnel involved in the die changeover process on the hydraulic press. It encompasses the necessary steps to ensure safety and quality in accordance with AIQS 3.2.3.

## 3. Procedure  
1. **Preparation**: Before initiating the die changeover, conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) to verify that the hydraulic press (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061) is in proper working condition. Ensure that all necessary tools and replacement dies are available.

2. **Lockout-Tagout (LOTO)**: Implement LOTO procedures to ensure the hydraulic press is de-energized and cannot be operated during the die changeover. Confirm that all personnel are aware of the LOTO status.

3. **Remove Existing Die**: Carefully remove the existing die from the hydraulic press. Inspect the die for any signs of wear or damage. If any issues are identified, complete a Nonconformance Report (NCR) as per AIQS 3.1.4.

4. **Install New Die**: Position the new die securely in the hydraulic press. Ensure proper alignment and fit. Perform a First Article Inspection (FAI) to verify that the die meets specifications.

5. **Final Checks**: Before re-energizing the hydraulic press, conduct a final inspection to ensure all components are secure and in place. Remove LOTO devices only after confirming that the area is clear and safe.

6. **Documentation**: Record the die changeover in the maintenance log, including any observations or issues encountered during the process, in compliance with AIQS 3.2.3.
