# WI-020: Final Inspection and Release Authorization

## 1. Purpose  
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the steps necessary for conducting final inspections and authorizing the release of manufactured products, ensuring compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) and maintaining product integrity per AIQS 3.3.1.

## 2. Scope  
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the final inspection process of products manufactured using equipment asset IDs 2847.510.0007 (Coordinate Measuring Machine) and 2847.310.0055 (Precision Surface Grinder). It encompasses the procedures for documenting results and addressing any Nonconformance Reports (NCR) as per AIQS 3.2.2.

## 3. Procedure  
1. Prepare for inspection by ensuring that all equipment, including the Coordinate Measuring Machine (2847.510.0007) and Precision Surface Grinder (2847.310.0055), is calibrated and ready for use. Perform Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures as necessary to ensure safety.

2. Conduct the First Article Inspection (FAI) on the initial product sample to verify that it meets the specified requirements. Document all measurements and findings in the inspection report, referencing AIQS 3.4.1 for compliance criteria.

3. If any discrepancies are found during the FAI, generate a Nonconformance Report (NCR) and initiate corrective actions as per AIQS 3.2.2. Ensure that all corrective actions are implemented and verified before proceeding.

4. Upon successful completion of the FAI and resolution of any NCRs, proceed with the final inspection of the production batch. Utilize the Coordinate Measuring Machine (2847.510.0007) for dimensional checks and the Precision Surface Grinder (2847.310.0055) for surface quality assessments.

5. Compile all inspection results and ensure they are reviewed and approved by a qualified supervisor. Final authorization for product release shall only occur after all inspection criteria have been met, in accordance with AIQS 3.3.1.
