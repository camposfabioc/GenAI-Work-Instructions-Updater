# WI-025: Internal Audit Preparation Procedure

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the steps necessary for preparing for an internal audit in compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This procedure ensures that all relevant documentation and processes are reviewed, promoting continuous improvement and adherence to quality standards per AIQS 1.2.4.

## 2. Scope
This procedure applies to all departments within the manufacturing plant that are subject to internal audits. It encompasses preparation activities, documentation review, and equipment checks to ensure compliance with AIQS requirements, specifically AIQS 1.3.3.

## 3. Prerequisites
Before initiating the internal audit preparation, personnel shall complete the following prerequisites: 
1. Review previous audit reports and Nonconformance Reports (NCRs) to identify areas of concern per AIQS 2.1.4.
2. Ensure that all relevant documentation, including Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), is up to date and accessible.
3. Conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) of equipment asset IDs 2847.420.0023 (Injection Molding Unit) and 2847.510.0007 (Coordinate Measuring Machine) to confirm operational status.
4. Verify that all personnel involved in the audit preparation are trained and familiar with the AIQS standards, particularly AIQS 1.3.4.

## 4. Procedure Step 1
Initiate the internal audit preparation by assembling the audit team. The team shall consist of members from various departments to ensure a comprehensive review. Assign roles and responsibilities to each team member, ensuring that all aspects of the audit process are covered. Schedule a kickoff meeting to discuss the audit objectives, scope, and timeline. Document the meeting minutes and distribute them to all team members. This step is critical for aligning the team with the audit goals and ensuring compliance with AIQS 1.2.1.

## 5. Procedure Step 2
Conduct a thorough review of all relevant documentation, including SOPs, work instructions, and previous audit findings. Each team member shall be responsible for reviewing specific documents related to their area of expertise. Identify any discrepancies or outdated information that may affect the audit outcome. Document findings and prepare a list of required updates or corrections. This review process is essential to ensure that all documentation meets the standards set forth in AIQS 2.2.3.

## 6. Procedure Step 3
Perform a physical inspection of the equipment involved in the audit, specifically the Injection Molding Unit (2847.420.0023) and the Coordinate Measuring Machine (2847.510.0007). Ensure that all equipment is operational and meets safety standards. Implement Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures as necessary during the inspection to ensure safety. Document any maintenance or repair needs identified during the inspection. This step is crucial to verify that equipment is compliant with operational standards as outlined in AIQS 2.1.4.

## 7. Procedure Step 4
Prepare the audit checklist based on the AIQS requirements and the specific processes of the departments being audited. The checklist shall include criteria for evaluating compliance, effectiveness, and areas for improvement. Distribute the checklist to all audit team members for review and feedback. Incorporate any suggestions to enhance the checklist's effectiveness. This checklist will serve as a vital tool during the audit, ensuring that all necessary aspects are covered per AIQS 1.3.3.

## 8. Procedure Step 5
Schedule the internal audit dates and notify all relevant personnel. Ensure that all departments are aware of the audit schedule and are prepared to participate. Conduct a pre-audit briefing with the audit team to review the audit process, expectations, and roles. Emphasize the importance of transparency and cooperation during the audit. Document the schedule and briefing notes, ensuring that all team members are aligned with the audit objectives as per AIQS 1.3.4.

## 9. Verification
After the internal audit is conducted, the audit team shall review all findings and prepare a summary report. This report shall include identified nonconformities, areas for improvement, and recommendations for corrective actions. Verify that all findings are documented accurately and that responsible parties are assigned to address each issue. Follow up on the implementation of corrective actions to ensure compliance with AIQS standards, particularly AIQS 2.1.4.

## 10. Records
Maintain records of all documentation related to the internal audit preparation, including meeting minutes, audit checklists, inspection reports, and summary reports. Ensure that these records are stored securely and are accessible for future audits and reviews. Retain records in accordance with the retention policy outlined in AIQS 1.2.1, ensuring compliance with quality management standards.
