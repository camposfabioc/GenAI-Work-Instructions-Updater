# WI-008: Supplier Qualification Documentation

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the process for qualifying suppliers to ensure they meet the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) requirements. This process is essential for maintaining product quality and compliance with AIQS standards, specifically per AIQS 1.1.4 and AIQS 1.5.4.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the supplier qualification process at Acme Industrial Manufacturing. It encompasses the evaluation, selection, and monitoring of suppliers to ensure adherence to quality standards as defined in AIQS 1.3.3 and AIQS 2.3.1.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel must complete training on supplier qualification processes and be familiar with AIQS standards, particularly AIQS 1.1.2 and AIQS 1.5.2. Additionally, personnel shall have access to the Supplier Qualification Checklist and the Supplier Evaluation Form. Prior to initiating the qualification process, ensure that the necessary tools and equipment, including the Precision Surface Grinder (2847.310.0055) and Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061), are operational and compliant with safety regulations. Conduct a Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure if maintenance is required on any equipment.

## 4. Procedure Step 1
Initiate the supplier qualification process by gathering preliminary information about potential suppliers. This includes obtaining their company profile, quality certifications, and references. Complete the Supplier Qualification Checklist to ensure all necessary information is collected. Assess the supplier's ability to meet AIQS standards, particularly focusing on their quality management system as per AIQS 1.4.4. Document any discrepancies or concerns that arise during this initial assessment. If the supplier does not meet the minimum requirements, a Nonconformance Report (NCR) shall be generated, and the supplier shall be notified of the deficiencies.

## 5. Procedure Step 2
Conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) with the potential supplier. This review shall include a site visit to evaluate their manufacturing processes, quality control measures, and overall operational capabilities. During the ORR, verify that the supplier's processes align with AIQS 1.5.4. Observe equipment usage and maintenance practices, ensuring that they follow industry standards. Document findings in the Supplier Evaluation Form, highlighting any areas that require improvement. If the supplier's operations do not meet the required standards, further discussions shall be held to address these issues.

## 6. Procedure Step 3
Request and review the supplier's First Article Inspection (FAI) reports for initial production runs. The FAI is critical for verifying that the supplier can produce parts that meet specified requirements. Ensure that the FAI documentation is complete and complies with AIQS 1.1.4. Compare the FAI results against the specifications outlined in the purchase order. If discrepancies are found, communicate with the supplier to resolve these issues before proceeding with further qualification steps. Document the review process and any corrective actions taken in the Supplier Evaluation Form.

## 7. Procedure Step 4
Evaluate the supplier's ongoing performance by establishing key performance indicators (KPIs) based on quality, delivery, and responsiveness. Monitor these KPIs regularly to ensure the supplier maintains compliance with AIQS 1.3.3. Conduct periodic audits and reviews of the supplier's processes and products. If performance issues arise, initiate corrective actions and document them in the Supplier Evaluation Form. Engage with the supplier to develop improvement plans, ensuring that they understand the importance of meeting AIQS standards.

## 8. Procedure Step 5
Finalize the supplier qualification process by compiling all documentation, including the Supplier Qualification Checklist, Supplier Evaluation Form, and any NCRs or corrective action plans. Review all documents to ensure completeness and compliance with AIQS 2.3.1. Submit the compiled documentation to the Quality Assurance Manager for approval. Once approved, communicate the qualification status to the supplier and provide them with any necessary feedback. Maintain an open line of communication for any future inquiries or requirements.

## 9. Verification
Verify that all steps in the supplier qualification process have been completed and documented according to the established procedures. Conduct a final review of the Supplier Evaluation Form and ensure all required signatures are obtained. Confirm that the supplier meets all AIQS standards before approval. If any discrepancies are identified during verification, initiate a review process to address these issues promptly.

## 10. Records
Maintain all records related to the supplier qualification process, including the Supplier Qualification Checklist, Supplier Evaluation Form, FAI reports, and any NCRs. These records shall be stored in the designated quality management system database for a minimum of five years, as per AIQS 1.5.2. Ensure that records are easily accessible for audits and reviews, and that they are updated as necessary to reflect any changes in supplier status or performance.
