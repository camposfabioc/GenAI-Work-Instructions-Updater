# WI-022: Coordinate Measuring Machine Probe Qualification

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to establish the process for qualifying the probes used in the Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) to ensure compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This qualification is crucial for maintaining measurement accuracy and reliability as per AIQS 2.2.1.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the qualification of CMM probes within the manufacturing facility. It encompasses the procedures necessary for ensuring that probes meet the required specifications and standards outlined in AIQS 2.5.2.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel must complete an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) before commencing probe qualification. Ensure that the CMM is calibrated and in good working condition, as specified in AIQS 2.2.3. Additionally, all safety protocols must be followed, including Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures for any equipment involved. Familiarity with Nonconformance Report (NCR) processes is required to document any deviations during the qualification process.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Gather necessary tools and equipment, including the CMM (Asset ID: 2847.420.0023) and the probes to be qualified. Ensure that the workspace is clean and free from obstructions.
   
2. **Initial Inspection**: Conduct a visual inspection of each probe for any signs of wear or damage. Document findings and take corrective actions as necessary, per AIQS 3.2.4.

3. **Calibration Check**: Perform a calibration check of the CMM using a certified standard. Record the calibration results and verify that they fall within acceptable limits as defined in AIQS 2.5.2.

4. **Probe Qualification**: Attach the probe to the CMM and execute a series of test measurements on a known reference part. Compare the measurements against the reference values. If discrepancies are found, initiate an NCR and investigate the cause.

5. **Documentation**: Upon successful qualification, complete the qualification report, including all measurement data and any corrective actions taken. Ensure that the report is filed in accordance with AIQS 2.2.1.

6. **Final Review**: Review the qualification report with a supervisor for approval. Ensure that all documentation is complete and accurate before proceeding to use the probe for production measurements.

## 5. Verification
Verification of the probe qualification shall be conducted by a designated quality assurance representative. This includes reviewing the qualification report and ensuring compliance with AIQS 2.2.3. Any nonconformances identified during this review shall be addressed immediately, and corrective actions documented in the NCR system. Regular audits of the qualification process shall be performed to maintain adherence to AIQS standards.
