# WI-023: NCR Closure and Effectiveness Verification

## 1. Purpose  
This Work Instruction outlines the process for closing Nonconformance Reports (NCRs) and verifying the effectiveness of corrective actions implemented. Compliance with this procedure ensures adherence to the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) and maintains the integrity of manufacturing operations per AIQS 4.5.1.

## 2. Scope  
This procedure applies to all personnel involved in the NCR process within the manufacturing plant. It encompasses the verification of corrective actions related to equipment such as the Hydraulic Press (Asset ID: 2847.420.0018) and the Injection Molding Unit (Asset ID: 2847.420.0023) as stipulated in AIQS 4.2.4.

## 3. Procedure  
1. Upon identification of a nonconformance, generate an NCR using the designated template. Ensure all relevant details are captured, including the nature of the nonconformance and affected equipment, per AIQS 5.4.1.  
2. Assign a responsible individual to investigate the NCR and determine root causes. Document findings and proposed corrective actions in the NCR log.  
3. Implement corrective actions following the Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures to ensure safety during equipment adjustments.  
4. Conduct a First Article Inspection (FAI) on the affected equipment, such as the Hydraulic Press (2847.420.0018) or Injection Molding Unit (2847.420.0023), to verify that corrective actions have resolved the nonconformance.  
5. Complete the NCR closure form, including verification results and any additional comments. Ensure the form is reviewed and approved by the Quality Assurance team as per AIQS 5.5.4.  
6. Maintain records of the NCR and associated documentation for future reference and audits, ensuring compliance with AIQS 4.5.1.
