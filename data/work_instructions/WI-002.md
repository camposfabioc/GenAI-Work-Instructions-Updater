# WI-002: Management Review Data Collection

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the process for collecting data necessary for the Management Review, ensuring compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This data collection supports continuous improvement and decision-making processes as per AIQS 1.1.3.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in data collection for the Management Review at the manufacturing facility. It encompasses data related to production performance, quality metrics, and operational efficiency, as mandated by AIQS 1.5.4.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel must complete training on data collection methods and the AIQS standards relevant to this process. The following documents must be reviewed prior to data collection: the latest Operational Readiness Review (ORR) report, Nonconformance Reports (NCRs), and First Article Inspection (FAI) results. Ensure that equipment asset IDs 2847.310.0042 (Horizontal CNC Machining Center) and 2847.310.0055 (Precision Surface Grinder) are operational and have undergone Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures as per AIQS 2.3.4.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Data Identification**: Identify the key performance indicators (KPIs) required for the Management Review. This includes production output, defect rates, and equipment downtime metrics, in accordance with AIQS 1.2.1.
   
2. **Data Collection**: Utilize the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) and Precision Surface Grinder (2847.310.0055) to gather relevant production data. Record the data in the designated format, ensuring accuracy and completeness.

3. **Data Verification**: Cross-check collected data against existing records, including ORR and NCR documents. Any discrepancies must be resolved before proceeding.

4. **Data Compilation**: Compile the verified data into a report format suitable for the Management Review. Ensure that all data is presented clearly and concisely, following the guidelines set forth in AIQS 2.5.4.

5. **Submission**: Submit the compiled report to the Quality Manager for review. Ensure that the submission is completed within the designated timeframe to facilitate timely Management Review meetings.

## 5. Verification
The Quality Manager shall verify the accuracy and completeness of the data collected as per this WI. A checklist based on AIQS 1.5.4 shall be utilized to ensure all required data points are included. Any identified issues must be addressed prior to the Management Review to maintain compliance with AIQS 2.3.4.
