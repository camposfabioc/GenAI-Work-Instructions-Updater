# WI-001: CMM Program Validation Procedure

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the procedure for validating Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) programs to ensure compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This validation process is critical for maintaining product quality and operational efficiency as per AIQS 4.1.3.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the validation of CMM programs at the manufacturing facility. It encompasses the use of equipment asset IDs 2847.310.0042 (Horizontal CNC Machining Center) and 2847.420.0023 (Injection Molding Unit) in the validation process, as referenced in AIQS 4.2.1.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel must complete the Operational Readiness Review (ORR) prior to initiating the CMM program validation. Ensure that all relevant documentation, including First Article Inspection (FAI) reports and Nonconformance Reports (NCR), are available for reference. Personnel shall also be trained in Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures to ensure safety during equipment operation, as required by AIQS 3.4.4. Additionally, familiarity with AIQS clauses 3.2.1, 3.2.3, and 3.3.4 is essential for compliance.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Review the CMM program and associated documentation, including FAI reports and NCRs. Ensure that the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) and Injection Molding Unit (2847.420.0023) are in a ready state for validation.
   
2. **LOTO Implementation**: Implement Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures on the equipment to ensure safety during the validation process. Confirm that all personnel involved are aware of the LOTO status.

3. **Initial Setup**: Load the CMM program into the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042). Perform a dry run of the program to verify that there are no errors in the code. Document any discrepancies as per AIQS 3.2.1.

4. **Validation Execution**: Execute the CMM program while monitoring the machine's performance. Collect data on critical dimensions and compare them against the specified tolerances. Ensure that all measurements are recorded accurately, as required by AIQS 3.2.3.

5. **Data Analysis**: Analyze the collected data to determine if the CMM program meets the required specifications. If discrepancies are found, initiate a Nonconformance Report (NCR) per AIQS 3.3.4 and take corrective actions as necessary.

6. **Final Review**: Upon successful validation, complete the validation documentation and submit it for review. Ensure that all records are maintained in accordance with AIQS 4.2.1.

## 5. Verification
Verification of the CMM program validation shall be conducted by a qualified supervisor. The supervisor shall review all documentation, including validation results and any NCRs issued. Ensure that all records are stored in compliance with AIQS 4.1.3 and are accessible for future audits.
