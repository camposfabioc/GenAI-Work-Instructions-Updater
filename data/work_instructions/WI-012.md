# WI-012: Nonconforming Material Quarantine Procedure

## 1. Purpose  
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the procedure for the identification, handling, and quarantine of nonconforming materials in accordance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This ensures that all nonconforming items are properly managed to prevent their unintended use and to maintain product quality per AIQS 4.1.2.

## 2. Scope  
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the manufacturing process at the Acme Industrial manufacturing plant. It covers the procedures for quarantining nonconforming materials identified during production, inspection, or testing, as mandated by AIQS 5.5.3.

## 3. Prerequisites  
Personnel must complete the Operational Readiness Review (ORR) training and be familiar with the AIQS requirements. Prior to executing this procedure, ensure that a Nonconformance Report (NCR) is generated for any identified nonconforming materials. Personnel must also be trained in Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures to safely handle equipment, including the Hydraulic Press (Asset ID: 2847.420.0018) and the Precision Surface Grinder (Asset ID: 2847.310.0055). Refer to AIQS 4.2.2 for additional training requirements.

## 4. Procedure  
1. Upon identification of a nonconforming material, immediately complete an NCR, documenting the nature of the nonconformance and any relevant details. Ensure the NCR is submitted to the Quality Assurance (QA) department.
2. Clearly label the nonconforming material with a designated quarantine tag, indicating the NCR number and the reason for quarantine. Use red tags for high-risk items and yellow tags for low-risk items as per AIQS 5.5.4.
3. Move the nonconforming material to the designated quarantine area, ensuring it is securely stored and separated from conforming materials. The quarantine area must be clearly marked and accessible only to authorized personnel.
4. Notify the QA department of the quarantined material and provide them with the NCR for further investigation. The QA team will assess the nonconformance and determine the appropriate corrective action.
5. Once corrective actions are implemented, conduct a First Article Inspection (FAI) if necessary, to validate the effectiveness of the corrective measures. Document the results and update the NCR accordingly.
6. Upon resolution, remove the quarantine tag and return the material to the production line or dispose of it as per the established procedures.

## 5. Verification  
The QA department shall conduct periodic audits of the quarantine process to ensure compliance with this WI and AIQS standards. Verification shall include reviewing NCRs, inspecting the quarantine area, and confirming that all nonconforming materials are properly documented and handled. Any discrepancies shall be addressed immediately to maintain compliance with AIQS 5.5.3.
