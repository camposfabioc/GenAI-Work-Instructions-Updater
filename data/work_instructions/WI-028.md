# WI-028: Pre-shift Equipment Inspection Checklist

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction is to outline the procedures for conducting a pre-shift equipment inspection checklist to ensure operational readiness and compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This process aims to identify potential issues before they affect production, thereby maintaining safety and quality standards per AIQS 2.4.3.

## 2. Scope
This instruction applies to all operators and maintenance personnel responsible for the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061) and the Hydraulic Press, 12-ton (Asset ID: 2847.420.0018). Compliance with this Work Instruction is mandatory to prevent Nonconformance Reports (NCR) and ensure adherence to AIQS 2.2.3.

## 3. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Before commencing the inspection, ensure that all safety protocols are followed, including Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures as outlined in AIQS 3.2.1. Gather the necessary tools and inspection forms.

2. **Inspection Checklist**:
   - For the Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061):
     - Check for visible signs of wear or damage on the machine components.
     - Verify that all safety guards are in place and functional.
     - Ensure that the coolant levels are adequate and free from contamination.
   - For the Hydraulic Press (2847.420.0018):
     - Inspect hydraulic hoses for leaks or signs of deterioration.
     - Confirm that the emergency stop button is operational.
     - Test the functionality of the pressure gauge.

3. **Documentation**: Record all findings on the inspection checklist. If any issues are identified, escalate them immediately by completing a Nonconformance Report (NCR) as per AIQS 3.3.1. Ensure that all completed checklists are submitted to the supervisor for review and retention in accordance with AIQS 2.2.3. Conduct a First Article Inspection (FAI) if significant repairs are required.
