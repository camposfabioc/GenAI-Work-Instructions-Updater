# WI-018: Fixture Setup for Multi-part Runs

## 1. Purpose
This Work Instruction (WI) outlines the procedure for setting up fixtures on the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) for multi-part runs. Adhering to this WI ensures compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) and promotes operational efficiency while minimizing the risk of nonconformance.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all operators and technicians involved in the setup of fixtures for multi-part production runs on equipment 2847.310.0042 and 2847.310.0055 (Precision Surface Grinder). Compliance with this WI is mandatory to maintain quality standards as outlined in AIQS.

## 3. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Before initiating the setup, conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) to ensure that equipment 2847.310.0042 is in proper working condition per AIQS 3.1.1. Verify that all necessary tools and fixtures are available and free from defects.

2. **Lockout-Tagout (LOTO)**: Implement LOTO procedures to ensure the safety of personnel during the setup process. Confirm that all energy sources are properly locked and tagged according to AIQS 3.4.4.

3. **Fixture Installation**: 
   - Secure the fixture to the machine bed of the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042). Ensure that it is aligned according to the specifications outlined in the engineering drawings.
   - Perform a First Article Inspection (FAI) on the initial part produced to verify that the fixture setup meets the required tolerances and specifications. Document results and any discrepancies in a Nonconformance Report (NCR) as per AIQS 2.2.4.
   - After confirming the FAI, proceed with the multi-part run. Monitor the setup throughout production to ensure ongoing compliance with quality standards.

4. **Completion**: Upon completion of the run, remove the fixture and perform a thorough inspection of both the fixture and the equipment. Report any issues encountered during the setup or production process in accordance with AIQS guidelines.
