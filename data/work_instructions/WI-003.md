# WI-003: Coolant Concentration Monitoring

## 1. Purpose
This Work Instruction outlines the procedure for monitoring coolant concentration in the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) and the Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061). Maintaining proper coolant concentration is essential to ensure optimal machining performance and adherence to the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) guidelines, specifically per AIQS 2.2.1.

## 2. Scope
This instruction applies to all operators and maintenance personnel involved in the operation and maintenance of the Horizontal CNC Machining Center and Vertical Turning Lathe. It ensures compliance with AIQS standards and promotes consistent quality in manufacturing processes, as stated in AIQS 3.1.2.

## 3. Prerequisites
Before performing coolant concentration monitoring, personnel must complete the following prerequisites:
1. Attend training on coolant management and AIQS compliance, per AIQS 2.2.4.
2. Ensure that the equipment is in a safe condition for operation by conducting a Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure, in accordance with AIQS 2.4.3.
3. Gather necessary tools and materials, including a refractometer, coolant sample containers, and personal protective equipment (PPE).
4. Review the latest Nonconformance Report (NCR) related to coolant issues, if applicable, to understand past discrepancies and corrective actions taken.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Confirm that the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) and Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061) are operational and have been subjected to an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) prior to monitoring.
2. **Sample Collection**: Collect a coolant sample from each machine. Ensure that the sample is taken from the coolant reservoir to obtain an accurate representation of the concentration.
3. **Measurement**: Use the refractometer to measure the coolant concentration. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for calibration and operation of the refractometer.
4. **Documentation**: Record the coolant concentration readings in the designated logbook. Include the date, time, machine ID, and measured concentration. This documentation is critical for compliance with AIQS 3.2.2.
5. **Adjustment**: If the coolant concentration is outside the acceptable range, adjust the coolant mixture as necessary. Refer to the coolant manufacturer's specifications for the correct dilution ratios.
6. **Final Check**: After adjustments, re-measure the coolant concentration to ensure it falls within the specified range. Document any adjustments made in the logbook.
7. **Reporting**: If any issues arise during monitoring or adjustments, escalate the situation by completing a Nonconformance Report (NCR) as per AIQS 3.4.1.

## 5. Verification
To verify compliance with this Work Instruction, supervisors shall conduct periodic audits of the coolant concentration logs and the maintenance of the Horizontal CNC Machining Center and Vertical Turning Lathe. Ensure that all documentation aligns with AIQS 3.1.2 and that corrective actions from any NCRs are implemented effectively. Regular reviews will help maintain adherence to AIQS 2.2.1 and ensure ongoing operational excellence.
