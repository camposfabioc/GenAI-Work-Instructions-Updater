# WI-011: Document Control for Quality Records

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to establish a standardized process for managing quality records in compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). This ensures that all records are accurate, accessible, and maintained to facilitate quality assurance and continuous improvement per AIQS 1.2.3.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all quality records generated during manufacturing processes, including but not limited to Operational Readiness Reviews (ORR), Nonconformance Reports (NCR), and First Article Inspections (FAI). It encompasses equipment asset IDs 2847.310.0055 (Precision Surface Grinder) and 2847.310.0061 (Vertical Turning Lathe) as part of the quality control process per AIQS 1.5.4.

## 3. Procedure
1. **Document Creation**: All quality records shall be created using the designated templates. Ensure that each record includes the date, equipment asset ID, and the responsible personnel's name. This aligns with the requirements outlined in AIQS 1.4.1.

2. **Document Review**: Quality records shall be reviewed by a designated quality assurance personnel within 48 hours of creation. The reviewer shall verify the accuracy of the information and ensure compliance with relevant standards. Any discrepancies shall be documented in an NCR and addressed promptly.

3. **Document Storage**: All quality records shall be stored in a secure electronic system, accessible only to authorized personnel. Each record must be tagged with a unique identifier for easy retrieval. Ensure that records are backed up weekly to prevent data loss, in accordance with AIQS 2.1.2.

4. **Document Retention**: Quality records shall be retained for a minimum of five years or as specified by regulatory requirements. After the retention period, records shall be disposed of securely to maintain confidentiality and compliance with AIQS standards. 

5. **Training**: Personnel involved in the creation, review, and storage of quality records shall receive training on this WI and the AIQS standards to ensure understanding and compliance.
