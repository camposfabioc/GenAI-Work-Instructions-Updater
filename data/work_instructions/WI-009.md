# WI-009: First Article Inspection Execution

## 1. Purpose  
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the procedure for conducting First Article Inspections (FAI) as per Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) guidelines. This ensures that all products meet specified requirements and are compliant with AIQS 3.3.3, thereby preventing defects and ensuring quality assurance.

## 2. Scope  
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the FAI process for products manufactured using equipment asset IDs 2847.420.0023 (Injection Molding Unit) and 2847.420.0018 (Hydraulic Press, 12-ton). It encompasses the preparation, execution, and documentation of the FAI in accordance with AIQS 3.4.4.

## 3. Procedure  
1. Prepare for the FAI by ensuring all necessary documentation is available, including design specifications and previous inspection records. Conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) to confirm that equipment asset ID 2847.420.0023 is operational and meets safety standards per AIQS 3.3.1.

2. Implement Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures on the Injection Molding Unit and Hydraulic Press before commencing the FAI. Verify that all safety measures are in place to protect personnel and equipment.

3. Execute the FAI by running a sample batch using both the Injection Molding Unit (2847.420.0023) and the Hydraulic Press (2847.420.0018). Measure critical dimensions and compare them against the specifications. Document any discrepancies and generate a Nonconformance Report (NCR) if necessary, in accordance with AIQS 3.4.4.

4. Upon successful completion of the FAI, compile all findings and submit the report to the Quality Assurance department for review. Ensure that all documentation is retained for future reference and compliance checks as mandated by AIQS 3.3.3.
