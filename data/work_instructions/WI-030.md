# WI-030: Vertical Turning Lathe Spindle Calibration

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the calibration process for the spindle of the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061). Proper calibration ensures that the spindle operates within specified tolerances, thereby maintaining product quality and compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS) requirements.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the calibration of the spindle on the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061). It encompasses the procedures necessary to achieve compliance with AIQS standards, specifically addressing calibration protocols and documentation requirements.

## 3. Prerequisites
Before commencing the calibration process, personnel shall complete an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) for the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061). Ensure that all necessary tools and equipment are available, including calibration gauges and measuring instruments. Personnel must also review the latest Nonconformance Report (NCR) related to spindle calibration and complete a First Article Inspection (FAI) if applicable. Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures must be implemented to ensure safety during the calibration process, as per AIQS 2.1.1.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Confirm that the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061) is powered down and properly locked out using LOTO procedures. Gather all required calibration tools and documentation.
   
2. **Initial Inspection**: Conduct a visual inspection of the spindle and surrounding components for any signs of wear or damage. Document any findings in accordance with AIQS 3.2.1.

3. **Calibration Setup**: Attach the calibration gauge to the spindle. Ensure that it is securely fastened and that all measurement points are accessible.

4. **Calibration Process**: 
   - Rotate the spindle and measure the runout at specified intervals. Record the measurements.
   - Compare the recorded values against the manufacturer’s specifications. If values exceed tolerance limits, adjustments shall be made to the spindle alignment.
   - Repeat the measurement process until all values are within acceptable limits.

5. **Documentation**: Complete the calibration log, detailing the measurements, adjustments made, and the final calibration status. Ensure compliance with AIQS 3.2.2 by retaining all calibration records for future audits.

## 5. Verification
After calibration, verify the spindle's performance by conducting a test run on the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061). Observe the operation for any irregularities. Document the results and ensure that all records are filed according to AIQS 3.3.2. If any discrepancies are noted, initiate a Nonconformance Report (NCR) as per AIQS 2.1.1.
