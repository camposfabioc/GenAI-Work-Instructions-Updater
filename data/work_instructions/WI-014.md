# WI-014: Lockout-Tagout for Press Maintenance

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to establish a standardized Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure for maintenance activities on equipment, specifically the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (Asset ID: 2847.310.0042) and the Vertical Turning Lathe (Asset ID: 2847.310.0061). This ensures the safety of personnel and compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS).

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all maintenance personnel performing LOTO procedures on the specified equipment. It encompasses all tasks requiring the isolation of energy sources to prevent accidental machine start-up, in accordance with AIQS requirements.

## 3. Prerequisites
Personnel shall complete an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) prior to performing maintenance. They must be trained in LOTO procedures and familiar with the AIQS, particularly AIQS 2.3.1, which addresses safety practices. Additionally, all maintenance personnel shall have access to the latest Nonconformance Report (NCR) and First Article Inspection (FAI) documentation relevant to the equipment being serviced. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) must be worn at all times during maintenance activities as per AIQS 2.5.1.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Preparation**: Notify all affected personnel of the scheduled maintenance. Review the equipment’s operational manual and previous NCRs to identify potential hazards.
2. **Shutdown**: Power down the Horizontal CNC Machining Center (2847.310.0042) and Vertical Turning Lathe (2847.310.0061) using the main control panel. Follow the shutdown procedures outlined in AIQS 3.1.1.
3. **Isolation**: Locate the energy sources for both machines. This includes electrical, hydraulic, and pneumatic systems. Disconnect all energy sources and ensure they are isolated.
4. **Lockout-Tagout**: Apply LOTO devices to all energy isolating devices. Use a personal lock and tag, clearly indicating the maintenance activity. Ensure compliance with AIQS 3.5.2 for proper labeling.
5. **Verification**: Attempt to operate the equipment controls to confirm that the energy has been effectively isolated. This step is critical to ensure safety as per AIQS 3.4.2.
6. **Maintenance**: Perform the required maintenance tasks on the equipment. Document any findings or issues encountered during the process.
7. **Re-energizing**: Once maintenance is complete, remove the LOTO devices in the reverse order of application. Notify affected personnel before re-energizing the equipment.

## 5. Verification
After completing the maintenance and LOTO procedure, the supervisor shall verify that all steps were followed correctly. This includes checking the maintenance documentation against the AIQS 2.2.4 requirements. A final inspection of the equipment shall be conducted to ensure it is safe for operation before returning it to service. Any discrepancies shall be reported immediately.
