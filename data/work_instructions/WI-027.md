# WI-027: Injection Molding Unit Purging Procedure

## 1. Purpose  
The purpose of this Work Instruction (WI) is to outline the procedure for purging the Injection Molding Unit (ID: 2847.420.0023) to ensure optimal performance and compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). Proper purging prevents contamination and maintains product quality, as mandated by AIQS 2.2.1.

## 2. Scope  
This procedure applies to all operators and maintenance personnel involved in the operation and maintenance of the Injection Molding Unit (ID: 2847.420.0023). It is essential to follow this WI to adhere to AIQS 2.4.1 and ensure that all processes align with quality management requirements.

## 3. Procedure  
1. **Preparation**  
   a. Conduct an Operational Readiness Review (ORR) to ensure the Injection Molding Unit is ready for purging.  
   b. Verify that the area is clear of personnel and materials, and perform Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) on the unit to ensure safety.  
   c. Gather necessary materials, including purge compound and cleaning tools.

2. **Purge Process**  
   a. Set the temperature of the Injection Molding Unit to the appropriate level for the purge compound, as specified in the equipment manual.  
   b. Load the purge compound into the hopper of the Injection Molding Unit.  
   c. Start the unit and allow the purge compound to flow through the system until the material exiting the nozzle is free of contamination. Document the process per AIQS 3.1.1.

3. **Post-Purge Actions**  
   a. Once purging is complete, perform a First Article Inspection (FAI) of the first few parts produced to ensure quality standards are met.  
   b. If any Nonconformance Report (NCR) is generated during this process, address it immediately per AIQS 2.5.4.  
   c. Clean the work area and remove LOTO devices only after confirming that the unit is safe to operate.
