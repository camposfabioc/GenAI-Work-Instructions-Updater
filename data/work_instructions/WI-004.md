# WI-004: CNC Machining Center Setup and Alignment

## 1. Purpose
This Work Instruction (WI) outlines the necessary steps for the setup and alignment of the CNC Machining Center to ensure compliance with the Acme Industrial Quality Standard (AIQS). Proper setup is essential for achieving optimal machining accuracy and maintaining product quality as per AIQS 3.4.2.

## 2. Scope
This WI applies to all personnel involved in the setup and alignment of CNC Machining Centers within the manufacturing facility. It encompasses procedures relevant to equipment asset IDs 2847.310.0061 (Vertical Turning Lathe) and 2847.420.0023 (Injection Molding Unit), ensuring adherence to AIQS standards.

## 3. Prerequisites
Before commencing the setup, personnel shall complete the following prerequisites: 
1. Review the latest version of the AIQS documentation, particularly AIQS 2.4.1 regarding equipment maintenance.
2. Complete the Operational Readiness Review (ORR) for the CNC Machining Center.
3. Ensure all necessary tools and equipment are available, including calibration tools and safety gear.
4. Conduct a Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure to ensure the equipment is safely powered down and secured.
5. Verify that all previous Nonconformance Reports (NCR) related to the CNC Machining Center have been addressed.

## 4. Procedure
1. **Initial Inspection**: Inspect the CNC Machining Center for any visible damage or wear. Document findings and address any issues as per AIQS 3.1.1.
2. **Setup**: 
   - Power on the CNC Machining Center and perform a system check.
   - Load the required tooling and fixtures as per the job specifications.
   - Ensure that all tools are calibrated according to the standards outlined in AIQS 3.2.1.
3. **Alignment**: 
   - Utilize precision measuring tools to align the spindle and workpiece. Adjust as necessary to achieve the specified tolerances.
   - Confirm alignment by performing a test run with a dummy workpiece.
4. **Documentation**: Record all setup parameters and alignment results in the setup log. Ensure that the log is accessible for future reference and audits.
5. **Final Checks**: Conduct a final inspection of the setup and alignment. Ensure compliance with AIQS standards before proceeding to the First Article Inspection (FAI).

## 5. Verification
After completing the setup and alignment, personnel shall verify the following:
1. All setup logs are completed and stored according to AIQS 3.4.2.
2. Conduct a review of the alignment results against the specified tolerances.
3. Ensure that any discrepancies are documented and addressed in accordance with AIQS 2.4.1.
4. Confirm that the CNC Machining Center is ready for production and meets all quality assurance requirements before initiating the FAI process.
